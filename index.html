Features

Everything a deal needs to close

Tether gives every party in the deal exactly what they need — no more, no less.

Pre-built DD task template
A proven due diligence checklist covering documents, financing, financial DD, legal, operations, marketing, team, and transition — loaded automatically for every deal.
Timeline auto-scales
Set your DD period — 30, 45, or 60 days — and every task due date adjusts proportionally. Your deal always stays in sync with your actual closing schedule.
Flag issues instantly
Flag any task as a red flag with a required note. Flagged tasks surface in the weekly report and the portfolio dashboard so nothing gets buried or missed.
Weekly automated report
Every week all participants receive an email with completed tasks, active flags with notes, and overdue items. Subject: "[Deal Name] Diligence Weekly Report."
Magic link access
Invite your attorney, broker, advisor, or ops team with one click. They receive a magic link — no account, no password, no friction. Click and they're in.
Portfolio dashboard
Advisors and brokers see every active deal in one view — progress, flags, overdue tasks, and days remaining in DD. No more digging to know where a deal stands.
